7 Steps to Glow & Go

About This Project

Team

A collaborative effort with Ha Minh Hoang, Nguyen Bao Han, and Thai Thuc Minh Huyen.

My role involved designing the infographic layout, creating illustrations, and developing the interactive elements using Figma.

Assignment

Students will work on an interactive progress/how-to type of infographic featuring 7-step instructions. The designs are optimised for desktop view (width 1440px) and should feature interactive elements and navigations.

Concept

At first glance, sanding down plywood and smoothing out your pores may seem worlds apart—but for skaters, they’re surprisingly intertwined. Skaters trip, slams into pavement, and blunder their faces—making self-care a necessity. This session dives into how to build your own custom skateboard from raw materials while also crafting a skincare routine tailored for active lifestyles through the aesthetics of a comic-like style.

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe InDesign
  • Figma
